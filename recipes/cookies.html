Cornmeal Cookies

Description

These are delicious and chewy cookies with a nice punch of cornmeal flavor

Ingredients

  • 1⁄2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3⁄4 cup firmly packed light brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1⁄8 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1⁄8 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 1⁄4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3⁄4 cup cornmeal
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon baking sode
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornmeal

Steps

  1. Mix Cookie Base
    1. Beat butter, brown sugar, and honey together on medium-high speed with an electric mixer for 5 minutes.
    2. Add in egg yolk, vanilla, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg and mix for 1 minute.
    3. Add in flour, 3/4 cup cornmeal, baking powder, and baking soda and mix until just combined.
  2. Prepare Cookie Coat
    • Mix 2 tablespoons white sugar and 1 tablespoon corn meal together in a small bowl.
  3. Shape Dough
    1. Scoop out 2 tablespoons of the dough and roll into a ball.
    2. oll dough ball into the sugar-corn meal mixture until thoroughly coated.
    3. Repeat with remaining cookie dough.
  4. Chill
    • Chill dough balls in the refrigerator for 15 minutes.
  5. Prepare Oven
    1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    2. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.
  6. Arrange Dough for Baking
    • Place dough balls 2 inches apart on the prepared baking sheets.
  7. Bake
    1. Bake in the preheated oven, one pan at a time, until cookies are crackled and begin to turn very light golden brown, 14 to 16 minutes.
    2. Cool cookies on baking sheets for 10 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
