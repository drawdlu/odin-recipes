Carbonara

Description

This is a simple Carbonara recipe which creates a super rich classic "bacon and egg" inspired dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided, or as needed
  • 8 slices bacon, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1⁄4 cup dry white wine
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 1⁄2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook pasta
    1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
    2. Cook spaghetti in boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes.
    3. Drain, toss spaghetti with 1 tablespoon olive oil, and set aside.
  2. Cook bacon
    1. Place diced bacon in a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
    2. Drain bacon on paper towels, reserving 2 tablespoons bacon fat in the skillet.
  3. Prepare sauce base
    1. Add 1 tablespoon olive oil to bacon fat in the skillet.
    2. Add chopped onion and cook over medium heat until onion is translucent.
    3. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add wine and cook 1 minute more.
  4. Combine cooked ingredients
    1. Return cooked bacon to the skillet; add cooked spaghetti.
    2. Toss to coat and heat through, adding more olive oil if it seems dry or sticks together.
    3. Add beaten eggs and cook, tossing constantly with tongs or a large fork, until eggs are barely set.
    4. Quickly add 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese and toss again.
    5. Season with salt and pepper (remember that bacon and Parmesan are very salty).
