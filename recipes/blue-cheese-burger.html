Blue Cheese Burger

Description

This is a cheeseburger with a twist. Instead of using the usual american cheese, we will be using blue cheese to bring out a different flavor combination

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1⁄2 cup minced fresh chives
  • 4 ounces blue cheese, crumbled
  • 1 1⁄2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon coarsely ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dry mustard
  • 1⁄4 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
  • 12 hamburger buns, split

Steps

  1. Prepare the patty
    1. Mix ground beef, chives, blue cheese, salt, Worcestershire sauce, pepper, mustard, and hot pepper sauce together in a large bowl until well combined.
    2. Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours.
  2. Prepare the oven
    • When ready to cook, preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  3. Form patties
    • Remove beef mixture from the refrigerator; form into 12 patties.
  4. Cook patties and assemble burger
    1. Cook patties on the preheated grill until an instant-read thermometer inserted into the center reads at least 160 degrees F (71 degrees C), about 5 minutes per side.
    2. Serve on hamburger buns.
